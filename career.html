Corporate Secretary

Governance

Job Description

  • Manage all corporate legal and administrative documents (deeds, permits, agreements).
  • Prepare agendas, invitations, and minutes of board meetings and partner meetings.
  • Maintain compliance with relevant regulations (OJK, Kemenkumham, taxation, etc.).
  • Draft and archive corporate resolutions, circulars, and other corporate documents.
  • Serve as the primary liaison with notaries, legal counsel, and regulators.
  • Ensure KYC, AML, and ethics protocols are upheld across all portfolio engagements.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience as Corporate Secretary, Legal Officer, or in Compliance — preferably in financial services, investment, or fiduciary industries.
  • Strong understanding of Indonesian corporate law, partnership structures, and OJK regulations.
  • Detail-oriented with strong documentation discipline.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills (Bahasa Indonesia & English).
  • Bachelor's degree in Law, Notarial Studies, or related field.
