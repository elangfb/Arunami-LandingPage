Investment Thesis

We plant early, and we plant with care.

i.

Ticket under IDR 1B

We invest at the seed stage—where the number on the cheque is smaller than the conviction behind it. Enough to buy runway, patient enough to grow into value.

ii.

Companies under three years

We meet founders in the formative season: when the product is finding its shape, the team is finding its rhythm, and the market is still being read—carefully.

iii.

Strong founders & teams

Not pedigree—clarity. Founders with the maturity to know that building is unromantic work, and the conviction to keep going when it is.

iv.

Rooted in fundamentals

Ideas that are exciting, yes—but anchored in profitability, real problems, and sustainable unit economics. We don't fund growth fuelled by euphoria.
